Volume Formulas
  • Cube: V = side³
  • Sphere: V = 4/3 × π × radius³
  • Cylinder: V = π × radius² × height
  • Cone: V = 1/3 × π × radius² × height
  • Rectangular Prism: V = length × width × height
  • Pyramid: V = 1/3 × base area × height
  • Hemisphere: V = 2/3 × π × radius³
  • Torus: V = 2π² × R × r²
